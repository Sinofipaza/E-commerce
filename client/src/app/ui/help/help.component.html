Welcome to the MerchDay Help Centre

We’re here to assist you! Browse through the topics below for quick answers to common questions or reach out to our support team if you need further help.

1. Orders and Payments

How do I place an order?

Simply browse our collection, select your desired item and size, add it to your cart, and proceed to checkout. Follow the prompts to enter your shipping and payment details.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit and debit cards, PayPal, and other secure payment methods displayed during checkout.

Can I modify or cancel my order after placing it?

Orders can only be modified or canceled within 24 hours of placement. Contact us immediately at support@merchday.com with your order number for assistance.

2. Shipping and Delivery

What are the shipping options and costs?

We offer standard and express shipping options. Costs vary based on your location and will be calculated at checkout.

How long does delivery take?

Delivery times depend on your location and shipping method: Standard Shipping: 5–10 business days.
Express Shipping: 2–5 business days.

How do I track my order?

Once your order ships, you’ll receive a tracking link via email. Use this link to monitor your shipment’s progress.

What should I do if my package is delayed or lost?

If your package hasn’t arrived within the expected timeframe, please email us at support@merchday.com. We’ll investigate and assist in resolving the issue.

3. Returns and Refunds

What is your return policy?

Items can be returned within 30 days of delivery if they are unused, unwashed, and in their original condition with tags. Check our Return Policy for details.

How do I initiate a return or exchange?

Email support@merchday.com with your order number and reason for the return. We’ll provide further instructions.

How long does it take to receive my refund?

Once we receive and inspect your return, refunds are processed within 5–7 business days to your original payment method.

4. Products and Sizing

Do you have a size guide?

Yes! Each product page includes a detailed size guide. Be sure to check it before ordering to ensure the best fit.

Are your T-shirts pre-shrunk or true to size?

Most of our T-shirts are true to size, but we recommend checking the product description for specific details.

What should I do if an item is out of stock?

Sign up for restock alerts on the product page by entering your email. We’ll notify you as soon as it’s back in stock.

5. Account and Privacy

Do I need an account to shop at MerchDay?

No, you can check out as a guest. However, creating an account lets you track orders and save your preferences for future purchases.

How do I create or delete my account?

To create an account, click on the “Sign Up” button at the top of our site. To delete your account, contact us at support@merchday.com.

How is my personal information protected?

We use industry-standard security measures to safeguard your data. For more details, check our Privacy Policy.

6. Promotions and Discounts

How do I use a discount code?

Enter your discount code at checkout in the designated field. The discount will be applied before you complete your purchase.

Can I combine multiple discount codes in one order?

Unfortunately, only one discount code can be applied per order.
